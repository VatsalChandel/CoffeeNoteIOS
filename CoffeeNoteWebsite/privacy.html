Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

CoffeeNote ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application CoffeeNote (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • Account credentials (securely hashed password)
  • User ID (automatically generated)

Location Information

CoffeeNote collects location data to provide core functionality:

  • Coffee shop locations: When you log a visit, we store the coffee shop's address and coordinates
  • Current location: Used to search for nearby coffee shops and calculate distances
  • Map features: Location data powers the map view showing your visited shops

Important: We only access your location when you actively use location features. We do not track your location in the background.

Usage Data

We collect information about your visits to coffee shops:

  • Coffee shop names and addresses
  • Ratings you provide (1-5 stars)
  • Items ordered and prices
  • Personal notes and comments
  • Visit dates
  • Wishlist items

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store your coffee shop visit history
  • Display your visits on a map (Premium feature)
  • Calculate statistics about your coffee journey
  • Show distance to wishlist locations
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the App and develop new features
  • Process subscription payments (through Apple)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform):

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication secures your account
  • Database: Cloud Firestore stores your visits and profile data
  • Security Rules: Only you can access your own data

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) hosts our backend infrastructure
  • Apple: For in-app purchase processing (they handle all payment data)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

We never share your location data with advertisers or data brokers.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all information we have about you
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data: Request a copy of your data (feature coming soon)
  • Opt-out of location services: Disable location access in iPhone Settings
  • Update your information: Edit or delete visits, notes, and profile data

To exercise these rights, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or contact us at support@coffeenote.app.

Children's Privacy

CoffeeNote is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

CoffeeNote uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and hosting
    Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps: Location search and map display
    Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple In-App Purchase: Subscription processing
    Apple Privacy Policy

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Sending you a notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@coffeenote.app

Website: www.coffeenote.app

Summary

Your privacy in plain English:

  • We collect your email, coffee shop visits, and location data
  • We use this data only to power the app's features
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • You can delete your account and all data anytime
  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely in Firebase
